## FAQ

### Q: Can I try before I buy?
A: Yes, we offer a 14-day free tier with limited access for evaluation.

### Q: What happens to my license if I exceed the dataset size?
A: We'll notify you and offer an upgrade path. No service interruption.

### Q: Can I get a refund?
A: Pilot programs can be cancelled with 30-day notice. Unused months are refunded.

### Q: Is there a minimum commitment?
A: Pilot: 1 month minimum. Full license: 3 months minimum.
